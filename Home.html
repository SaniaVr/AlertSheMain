Supporting Assault Survivors Together

At AlertShe, we believe in the power of connection and community. We are an innovative online platform dedicated to providing a safe and secure space for women to communicate, share experiences, and access crucial resources. Designed to support women in emergency situations, AlertShe offers a range of features aimed at empowering users to seek help, connect with others, and find solace in a supportive community.

Utilizing blockchain technology, specifically the Polygon network, AlertShe ensures that all posts are immutable and tamper-proof. This guarantees the authenticity and integrity of the content shared on the platform, providing users with confidence that their stories and reports remain unchanged and secure.

Protecting Ourselves from Crimes

In the 21st century, it is crucial for women to know how to defend themselves, both physically and mentally, against potential threats and violence. As society continues to evolve, the prevalence of harassment, assault, and gender-based violence remains a significant concern. Empowering women with self-defense skills not only enhances their personal safety but also boosts their confidence and autonomy.

AlertShe plays a pivotal role in this empowerment by providing a secure and supportive platform where women can share their stories and experiences without fear of censorship or tampering. Leveraging blockchain technology, AlertShe ensures that every post is immutable and authentic, creating a trustworthy space for women to connect, find solidarity, and access resources. This secure environment fosters open communication and helps build a community where women can seek advice, share coping strategies, and support one another in their journey towards recovery and empowerment.

Our Story

AlertShe was founded by a group of dedicated individuals passionate about women's safety and technology. Inspired by the need for a trustworthy platform where women can report incidents and access safety resources without fear of tampering, we created AlertShe. By leveraging blockchain technology, we aim to provide a secure space for women to share their experiences and support each other.
